About Me

I've been doing music in different capacities for the last 20 years. My skills include music theory, singing, saxophone, violin, piano and music production. I believe the voice is the first instrument, and began singing as a child. I later picked up the piano, and violin, as well as music theory to formalize my music education. At the time, I was focused on classical music, playing in Orchestras and a more operatic type of singing. I played the violin for about 8 years before picking up the saxophone in 2018 to learn more about Jazz. I'm currently working on creating more experimental music that fuses my favourite genres such as Jazz, World Music, Blues and Classical.
